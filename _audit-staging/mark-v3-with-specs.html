How it works

A simple path to getting started

Therapy is a big decision. We try to make the first few steps small.

01

Reach out

Send a message or book a free 15-minute consultation. Tell us what is going on, in whatever words feel right.

02

Find your fit

We talk briefly about what you are looking for and whether one of our clinicians is the right match. If we are not, we will try to point you somewhere that is.

03

Begin the work

Your first session is a longer intake. We listen, ask questions, and start mapping out what we will work on together.
