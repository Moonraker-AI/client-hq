The CORE Strategy for Your Practice

Every piece of the CORE strategy we build for you is designed to work together. Fixing your indexing issues makes your content findable. Adding schema and service page depth teaches AI what you do. Building your bio page and social presence proves you're qualified to do it. And tightening your hero section and CTAs turns that new visibility into real consultations. Here's how each pillar addresses what we found.

Credibility: Prove You're Real

We'll start by resolving the redirect-based indexing errors Google has flagged on your Wix site so your pages are actually being seen. From there, we'll add DKIM and DMARC records to your domain, build out your directory presence across 15 core citations plus all 5 major data aggregators, and launch your interconnected social profiles on platforms like LinkedIn, YouTube, Facebook, and Instagram. Together, these steps create the kind of verifiable digital footprint that causes AI systems to treat your practice as a trusted, real-world entity rather than an anonymous webpage.

Optimization: Make AI Understand You

We'll build or significantly expand five dedicated service pages targeting your most important specialties, including C-PTSD, EMDR, trauma therapy, first responder therapy, and men's therapy. Each page will include a proper H1 heading, a keyword-rich opening paragraph, service-specific FAQs drawn from real search data, and schema markup so AI can read and cite the content directly. We'll also create location pages for Long Island and Florida to make your geographic coverage explicit. A general FAQ page covering your policies, session format, and private-pay details will round out the structure.

Reputation: Amplify the Signal

We'll build a fully structured bio page that consolidates your credentials, license verification link, NPI number, clinical training, education, and the peer endorsement Jennifer Goggin has already written. This page gives AI a single, authoritative source to pull from when evaluating your qualifications. We'll also set up your YouTube channel and produce a professional intro video and supporting content to establish a visible, human presence. Press releases and NEO images will add additional third-party signals that reinforce your expertise in trauma and EMDR therapy on Long Island.

Engagement: Convert Visitors to Clients

We'll refine your hero section so that the first thing a visitor sees immediately communicates who you help, how you help them, and how to take the next step. Your existing consultation CTA is a great starting point, and we'll make sure it's reinforced throughout the page with clear, low-pressure language that feels aligned with your clinical voice rather than salesy. The goal is that someone who lands on your site from an AI recommendation feels immediately confident that they've found the right therapist and knows exactly how to reach you.
