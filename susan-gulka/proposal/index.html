Name confusion is actively working against you. Multiple businesses operating under names similar to "Heartwise Counseling" are creating ambiguity for Google and AI systems. When search engines can't confidently distinguish your practice from others with similar names, they're less likely to recommend you - and potential clients searching for you specifically may land somewhere else.

🔴

Inconsistent NAP data across the web is a serious credibility gap. Your name, address, and phone number appear in different formats across various directories and listings. This inconsistency signals uncertainty to Google's verification systems and makes it much harder for AI platforms like ChatGPT and Gemini to confidently cite your practice in local recommendations.

⚠️

Social profiles appear limited or unconnected. A robust, interconnected set of social profiles - LinkedIn, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, and others - signals to Google that a real human-operated practice exists behind your website. Without these connected profiles pointing back to HeartWise, your digital footprint remains thin and easy for AI to overlook.

⚠️

DNS authentication records may not be in place. DKIM and DMARC records create a technical security handshake with Google that proves your domain is tied to an actively operated practice. These are small but meaningful signals that help separate legitimate practices from automated spam - and they're often missing from solo practice websites.

⚠️

Your website likely lacks dedicated service pages for each specialty. AI systems learn what a practice treats by finding clearly structured, standalone pages for each service or modality. A single general "services" page doesn't give Google or Gemini enough signal to recommend you for specific searches like "anxiety therapy Lake Oswego" or whatever your primary specialties are.

🔴

Schema markup does not appear to be implemented. Schema is structured code that tells AI exactly what type of business you are, what services you offer, where you're located, and who you are as a clinician. Without it, AI has to guess - and guessing often means getting overlooked in favor of practices whose data is clearly labeled.

⚠️

A dedicated bio page for you as the clinician is likely missing. AI systems increasingly want to verify that a real, licensed professional is behind a practice. A well-structured bio page with your credentials, license verification links, NPI number, education, and professional affiliations is one of the highest-impact pages a solo practice can have.

⚠️

FAQ content appears limited or absent. Service-specific and practice-wide FAQ sections are among the fastest ways to get cited by AI in response to questions. "People Also Ask" style questions matched to your specialties can position HeartWise as the answer people are already searching for.

🔴

No visible professional endorsements or peer validation found. AI systems treat third-party endorsements - from colleagues, supervisors, or professional peers - as strong trust signals. Without verifiable endorsements on your bio page or website, AI has no way to confirm that others in the field vouch for your expertise.

🔴

No YouTube presence detected. A YouTube channel with short, informative videos is one of the most powerful reputation signals available to therapists today. Video content tied to your name and practice builds authority in a way that text alone cannot - and Google's AI systems weight video-based expertise heavily.

⚠️

No press features, media mentions, or directory spotlights identified. Third-party mentions - even simple press releases published to news distribution networks - act as external validation that HeartWise is a recognized, operating practice. These are meaningful signals for AI visibility and help differentiate you from practices with no external footprint.

⚠️

The path from visitor to booked client may not be clearly defined. Even with strong traffic, practices lose potential clients when the next step isn't immediately obvious. A compelling hero section with a single, clear call to action - paired with trust signals above the fold - makes a meaningful difference in how many visitors actually reach out.

⚠️

Calls to action may not be prominent or repeated throughout the site. Prospective therapy clients often need multiple nudges before they feel ready to reach out. Strategic placement of CTAs tied to specific services - not just a generic "contact me" at the bottom - keeps the door open at every stage of their visit.

The CORE Strategy for Your Practice

For HeartWise Counseling Services, the most urgent priorities are clarifying your identity online and establishing Susan as a verified, credible entity that AI platforms can confidently recommend. The name confusion and inconsistent contact data you're dealing with are solvable - but they require a systematic approach that addresses the root cause, not just surface-level edits. The CORE Marketing System works from the ground up: first proving you exist and are qualified, then making sure AI understands exactly what you offer in Lake Oswego, then amplifying that message with external signals, and finally ensuring every visitor who lands on your site has a clear and welcoming path to book a session.

Credibility: Prove You're Real

We'll start by resolving the name confusion issue head-on - standardizing your NAP (name, address, phone) across 15+ directory listings and all five major data aggregators so Google sees one consistent, authoritative record for HeartWise Counseling Services in Lake Oswego. We'll build out a full suite of interconnected social profiles and configure DNS authentication records to signal that a real, active practice operates behind your domain. This foundation is what makes everything else work.

Optimization: Make AI Understand You

We'll build dedicated service pages for your top five specialties, each written with proper heading structure, target keywords in the opening content, and service-specific FAQs drawn from what people are already searching. We'll implement schema markup so AI systems know exactly what you treat, where you practice, and who you are. A structured bio page for you - complete with credentials, license links, and professional affiliations - will give Gemini and ChatGPT the verifiable professional details they need to recommend you with confidence.

Reputation: Amplify the Signal

We'll establish a YouTube channel and playlist tied to your most important keywords, create professional NEO images that reinforce your identity across platforms, publish press releases to signal external recognition, and build out peer endorsements on your bio page. Each of these pieces acts as a third-party signal that HeartWise is not only real, but respected - the kind of practice AI platforms feel confident recommending to someone in a vulnerable moment of seeking help.

Engagement: Convert Visitors to Clients

We'll review and optimize your hero section and primary call to action to ensure the first thing a visitor sees clearly communicates who you help and how to take the next step. We'll ensure CTAs are present and prominent throughout the site - especially on service pages where intent is highest. The goal is to make sure that every person who finds HeartWise online has a frictionless, welcoming path to reach out and book a session with you.
